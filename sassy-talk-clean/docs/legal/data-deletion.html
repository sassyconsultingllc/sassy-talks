Data Deletion Request

Good news! Sassy-Talk does not store any personal data on external servers. All app data is stored locally on your device only.

How to Delete Your Data

Since Sassy-Talk stores all data locally on your device, you can completely remove all app data by following these steps:

Android

  1. Go to Settings on your device
  2. Tap Apps or Applications
  3. Find and tap Sassy-Talk
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear Data to remove all app data
  6. Optionally, tap Uninstall to completely remove the app

Windows/macOS/Linux

  1. Uninstall Sassy-Talk through your system's app manager
  2. All local data is automatically removed with uninstallation

What Data Is Deleted

  • Device identifier (randomly generated)
  • App settings (volume, channel preferences)
  • Cached peer discovery data

Data We Never Store

  • Voice recordings or transcripts
  • Personal information
  • Location data
  • Communication history
  • Any data on external servers
