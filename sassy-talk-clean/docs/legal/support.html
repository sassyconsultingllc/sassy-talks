Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect to other devices?

Ensure all devices are on the same WiFi network. Tap "SCAN" to discover nearby devices. Both devices must be on the same channel (1-16) to communicate.

Why can't I hear the other person?

Check that:

  • Both devices are on the same channel
  • Volume is turned up on both devices
  • The speaker icon shows you're receiving
  • You're on the same WiFi network

How do I change channels?

Use the ▲ and ▼ buttons next to the channel display. There are 16 channels available (like a real walkie-talkie).

Is my communication secure?

Yes. All voice data is encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption. Communications are peer-to-peer and never pass through external servers.

Does this work without WiFi?

The app requires a network connection. On Android, Bluetooth direct communication is available when both devices support it.

How do I use Push-to-Talk?

Press and hold the large PTT button to transmit. Release to stop transmitting and listen for responses.

What's the range?

Range depends on your WiFi network coverage. As long as devices can reach each other on the network, communication works.

Does the app use my data/cellular?

No. Communication is local network only. No data is sent to the internet.

Troubleshooting

App crashes on startup

Try uninstalling and reinstalling the app. Ensure your device meets minimum requirements (Android 8.0+).

Microphone not working

Check that you've granted microphone permission. Go to Settings > Apps > Sassy-Talk > Permissions > Microphone.

No devices found when scanning

Ensure all devices are on the same WiFi network (not using mobile data). Some corporate networks may block multicast traffic.
