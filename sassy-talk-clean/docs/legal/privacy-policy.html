Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2025

Sassy Consulting LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Sassy-Talk application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Device Name: A user-configurable name for identifying your device to other users
  • Audio Data: Voice transmissions sent during push-to-talk sessions (not stored, transmitted in real-time only)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifier: A randomly generated ID for peer discovery (not linked to personal identity)
  • Network Information: Local IP address for peer-to-peer communication

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Enabling peer-to-peer voice communication
  • Discovering nearby devices on the same network
  • Encrypting communications for security

Data Storage and Transmission

  • Local Only: All data remains on your device and local network
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not transmit data to external servers
  • End-to-End Encryption: Voice data is encrypted using AES-256-GCM
  • No Recording: Audio is transmitted in real-time and not stored

Third-Party Services

Sassy-Talk does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Sassy-Talk is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for all voice data
  • X25519 key exchange for secure sessions
  • No persistent storage of communications

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all local data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
