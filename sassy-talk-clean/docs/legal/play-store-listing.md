📻 **Sassy-Talk - The Modern Walkie-Talkie**

Remember the joy of walkie-talkies? Sassy-Talk brings that retro experience to your smartphone with modern security and reliability.

**🎯 SIMPLE TO USE**
• Press and hold to talk, release to listen
• 16 channels just like real walkie-talkies
• Automatic device discovery on your network
• No accounts, no setup, no hassle

**🔒 PRIVACY FIRST**
• End-to-end encryption (AES-256-GCM)
• No cloud servers - direct device-to-device
• No data collection or tracking
• No ads, ever

**📡 WORKS ANYWHERE**
• WiFi networks (home, office, events)
• Bluetooth direct connection (Android)
• No internet required
• No range limits on your network

**🎨 RETRO STYLE**
• Authentic walkie-talkie interface
• LCD-style display
• Satisfying push-to-talk feedback
• Dark mode support

**PERFECT FOR:**
• Family communication at home
• Event coordination
• Gaming voice chat
• Office quick-talk
• Outdoor activities
• Anyone who misses walkie-talkies!

**TECHNICAL SPECS:**
• Opus audio codec for crystal-clear voice
• 20ms latency for real-time communication
• UDP multicast for instant discovery
• X25519 key exchange for security
