📱 Sassy-Talk: True Privacy by Design

We don't collect your data because we can't see it.

Sassy-Talk is a 100% peer-to-peer encrypted walkie-talkie app. Your conversations go directly from your device to your friend's device via Bluetooth. No servers. No cloud. No data collection. Period.

🚫 What We DON'T Collect

Unlike most apps, we genuinely collect ZERO personal data:

  • ❌ No names or email addresses
  • ❌ No conversation recordings
  • ❌ No location tracking
  • ❌ No contact list access
  • ❌ No analytics or usage statistics
  • ❌ No advertising IDs
  • ❌ No user accounts
  • ❌ No third-party data sharing

🔐 How It Works

[Your Microphone] ↓ (Capture voice) [Local Encryption - AES-256] ↓ (Bluetooth direct connection) [Peer Device] ↓ (Decryption) [Peer Speaker] ↓ (Instant discard - NO storage)

📋 Permissions Required

🎤 RECORD_AUDIO

Why we need it: To capture your voice when you press the Push-to-Talk button

What happens: Audio is transmitted directly to your peer via Bluetooth

Storage: Audio is NEVER saved anywhere

📡 BLUETOOTH (Connect, Scan, Admin)

Why we need it: To connect directly to your friend's device

What happens: Only paired device info is accessed (standard Bluetooth)

Data transmitted: Only encrypted voice to your paired device

🌐 INTERNET (Declared but Unused)

Why it's declared: Reserved for potential future WiFi Direct features

Current usage: NONE - the app works 100% offline

Your privacy: No internet connection is made

🔒 Security & Encryption

  • ✅ AES-256-GCM Encryption - Military-grade end-to-end encryption
  • ✅ Local Keys Only - Encryption keys never leave your device
  • ✅ No Man-in-the-Middle - Direct Bluetooth connection only
  • ✅ Instant Deletion - Audio discarded after playback
  • ✅ Open Architecture - Code can be audited for security
